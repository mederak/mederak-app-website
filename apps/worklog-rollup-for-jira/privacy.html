Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Worlog Rollup for Jira handles data when used with Jira Cloud.

Who we are

Worlog Rollup for Jira is provided by Piotr Mederak, ul. Magnolii 1K/2, 05-500 Nowa Wola, Poland. For privacy or support requests, contact support.jira@mederak.pl.

Data we process

The app processes Jira issue data needed to calculate logged time rollups. This can include issue keys, summaries, issue types, parent relationships, issue counts, hierarchy depth and Jira time tracking values.

Jira data access

The app accesses Jira data through Atlassian Forge and Jira REST APIs using permissions granted during installation. It reads issue hierarchy and worklog time data needed to display the rollup panel.

Forge hosting and vendor access

The app is designed as an Atlassian Forge app. Product compute and storage are hosted on Atlassian infrastructure, not on a separate vendor-operated product backend. The app creator does not receive customer Jira worklog content on an external product server.

Data storage

Short-lived calculation cache is stored in app-scoped Forge Storage to reduce repeated calculations during normal page refreshes. Cache records are scoped to the app installation.

Data retention

Calculation cache is retained only as needed to provide the product functionality. Customers may request deletion of app-stored data by contacting support.

Website analytics and advertising measurement

This marketing website may use Google Analytics and Google Ads measurement through the Google tag. These tools are used to understand website performance, product page engagement and advertising campaign results. Optional analytics and advertising storage are controlled through the website consent banner and Google Consent Mode. Website analytics does not give Mederak Apps access to customer Jira worklog content or app data inside Jira Cloud.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is processed to provide the app and may be handled by Atlassian infrastructure as part of Forge and Jira Cloud. See Sub-processors for the current sub-processor summary.

Security

We apply privacy-conscious design principles: data minimization, Forge-hosted execution and avoiding unnecessary logging of Jira issue content. See Security Practices for more detail.

Your rights and requests

For access, correction, deletion or privacy questions, contact support.jira@mederak.pl. We may need tenant, site URL and app installation context to process a request.
