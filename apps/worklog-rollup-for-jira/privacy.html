Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Worklog Rollup for Jira handles data in Jira Cloud.

Who we are

Worklog Rollup for Jira is provided by Piotr Mederak, ul. Magnolii 1K/2, 05-500 Nowa Wola, Poland. Contact: support.jira@mederak.pl.

Data processed

The app processes Jira issue data needed to calculate logged time, including issue keys, summaries, issue types, parent relationships and Jira time tracking values.

Storage

The app may store short-lived calculation cache in Forge Storage. Cache records are used to reduce repeated calculations and are scoped to the app installation.

Vendor access

The app is designed as an Atlassian Forge app. Product compute and storage run in the Forge/Jira Cloud context rather than a separate vendor-operated product backend.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Atlassian provides Jira Cloud, Forge runtime, app storage and Marketplace installation context. See Sub-processors.

Requests

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact support.jira@mederak.pl.
