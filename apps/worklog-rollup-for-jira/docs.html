User guide

Documentation

Worklog Rollup for Jira shows logged time for the current issue and its descendants.

Quick start

  1. Open a Jira Cloud issue.
  2. Open the Worklog Rollup panel. In Jira it may be labeled Worklog Rollup.
  3. Review total logged time in MH and MD.
  4. Check the details table to see included issues with logged time.
  5. Use Refresh when you want to recalculate after recent worklog changes.

What is included

The app reads logged time from the current issue and descendant issues found through Jira's standard parent relationship. It uses Jira time tracking fields such as timespent and timetracking.timeSpentSeconds.

What is not included

The app does not calculate costs, rates, billing amounts or Tempo data. It is focused on Jira worklog time visibility.

Units

Time is shown in MH and MD. In the current product model, 1 MD equals 8 MH.

Refresh and cache

The app may use short-lived Forge Storage cache to avoid repeated calculations during normal page refreshes. The Refresh action requests a fresh calculation.

Large hierarchies

For very large issue trees, the app may stop at safety limits and show that the result was truncated. The summary then reflects the retrieved data.
