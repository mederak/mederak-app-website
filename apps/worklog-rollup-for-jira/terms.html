Terms

These terms apply to Worlog Rollup for Jira.

Use of the app

The app is provided for Jira Cloud teams that need visibility into logged time across issue hierarchies. Users are responsible for their Jira configuration, permissions and worklog data quality.

Marketplace installation and billing

Installation, evaluation, billing and subscription management are handled through Atlassian Marketplace and Atlassian account systems. Marketplace plan details are available from the app listing.

Availability

The app depends on Jira Cloud, Atlassian Forge and Jira APIs. Availability can be affected by Atlassian services, customer Jira configuration, user permissions and issue hierarchy size.

No financial calculation

The app does not calculate costs, rates, billing values or invoices. Displayed values are time summaries only.

Customer responsibilities

Customers are responsible for maintaining Jira permissions, time tracking configuration, worklog accuracy and issue hierarchy structure in their Jira Cloud site.

Support

Support is available through Mederak Apps Service Desk. Include the Jira site context, issue key and a description of the observed behavior.
