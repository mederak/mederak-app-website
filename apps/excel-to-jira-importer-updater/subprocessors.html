Sub-processors

This page lists core service providers involved in providing Excel to Jira Importer & Updater.

Atlassian

Jira Cloud, Atlassian Forge hosting, app runtime, app storage, Marketplace installation and authentication context.

Customer Jira site

Customer-controlled Jira Cloud environment where project data, created issues and issue properties are stored.

Google

Google Analytics, Google Ads and Google tag services for website measurement, campaign attribution and consent-aware advertising measurement on this marketing website.

Changes

Material changes to sub-processors will be reflected on this page. Customers can contact Mederak Apps Service Desk with questions.

Website analytics

Google measurement applies to this public marketing website only. It is separate from the Forge app runtime and does not process customer Excel files or Jira issue content from the installed app.
