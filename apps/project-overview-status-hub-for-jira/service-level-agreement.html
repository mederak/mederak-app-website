Support Service Level Agreement

This page describes target support response times. Response targets are not guaranteed resolution times.

Severity Meaning Target first response
Critical The app is unavailable for many customers or causes a severe production-impacting failure. 1 business day
High A core feature is broken for a customer, with no reasonable workaround. 1 business day
Medium A feature is impaired, but a workaround exists or the impact is limited. 2 business days
Low Question, documentation issue, cosmetic issue or low-impact enhancement request. 3 business days

Response versus resolution

Some issues depend on Atlassian platform behavior, customer configuration, missing diagnostic information or Marketplace review processes. Response targets describe initial support acknowledgement and triage, not guaranteed resolution time.

Security vulnerabilities

Security vulnerabilities are handled according to the Security Incident Response page and may follow a different internal remediation schedule based on severity.
