Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Project Overview & Status Hub for Jira handles data when used with Jira Cloud.

Who we are

Project Overview & Status Hub for Jira is provided by Piotr Mederak, ul. Magnolii 1K/2, 05-500 Nowa Wola, Poland. For privacy or support requests, contact support.jira@mederak.pl.

Plain English summary

The app reads Jira project information that Jira allows it to access, turns that information into project overviews and portfolio views, and stores app-specific overviews and settings in Atlassian Forge app storage for the customer's Jira site.

The app does not use a separate vendor-hosted database for project overviews. It does not change Jira issue statuses. It does not read data that Jira permissions do not allow it to read.

Data the app may access from Jira

  • Jira project identifiers, project keys, project names, project types and project leads.
  • Jira issue identifiers, issue keys, summaries, issue types, statuses, status categories, dates, due dates, links and recent activity.
  • Jira user display names and Atlassian account identifiers when needed for ownership, project roles, assignees, reporters, commenters or last-updated-by information.
  • Project roles, project permissions and app-specific global permissions used to control access.
  • Links discovered from Jira activity, such as documentation, dashboards, repositories, team channels, environments or support links.

Data stored by the app

The app may store saved project overviews, generated summaries, suggested people and links, radar signals, portfolio snapshots, refresh state, settings, section access rules, AI usage counters and temporary generation job records in Forge app storage.

AI-assisted features

The app may use Atlassian-hosted Forge LLM capabilities to help write readable project purpose suggestions and radar narratives. AI output is shown as a suggestion or summary and should be reviewed by users before it is treated as official project context.

Product analytics

The app may send sanitized product analytics events to Google Analytics when analytics is configured. Analytics payloads are designed not to include Jira issue text, issue keys, project names, user display names, email addresses, comments, links or saved overview text.

This marketing website may also use Google Analytics and Google Ads measurement through the Google tag. Website analytics does not give Mederak Apps access to customer Jira content or app data inside Jira Cloud.

Data retention

App records are retained while needed to provide product functionality, show saved overviews, support portfolio views and keep refresh settings available. Records may be overwritten by normal app use, removed by uninstalling the app according to Atlassian platform behavior, or deleted after a support request where technically feasible.

Sharing and subprocessors

We do not sell personal data. The app relies on Atlassian to host and operate Jira Cloud, Forge app hosting and Forge app storage. If product analytics is configured, the app may use Google Analytics for sanitized product telemetry. See Subprocessors for details.

Your rights and requests

For access, correction, deletion or privacy questions, contact support.jira@mederak.pl. We may need to work with the customer's Jira administrator because app records are associated with the customer's Atlassian site.
