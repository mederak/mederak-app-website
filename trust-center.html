About Mederak Apps

Mederak Apps builds focused Jira Cloud apps for common Jira workflows such as project status overviews, portfolio status signals, Excel-based issue imports and Jira worklog visibility. The apps are designed to be practical, clear to operate and documented for Marketplace buyers and administrators.

Security approach

Mederak Apps designs products with data minimization, Jira permission awareness and Atlassian Cloud platform capabilities in mind. Where applicable, apps use Atlassian Forge architecture and avoid a separate vendor-hosted product backend for customer app content.

  • Access is handled through Atlassian and Jira Cloud authentication.
  • App behavior is documented on product-specific security pages.
  • App scopes and storage behavior are described per product.
  • Security reports can be sent to the support contact listed on this page.

Privacy and data handling

Product privacy pages describe what each app processes and stores. Because Mederak Apps products support different Jira workflows, privacy and data handling details are maintained at product level rather than hidden behind a single generic description.

Support and security reporting

For product questions, support requests, Marketplace-related inquiries or security reports, contact Mederak Apps Service Desk. Please include the app name, Jira site context, affected workflow and reproduction steps where relevant. Do not include secrets, credentials or unnecessary customer data.
